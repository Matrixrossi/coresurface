About PaveVision

PaveVision is an innovative mobile app that leverages cutting-edge AI technology to automatically detect cracks, potholes, and other types of road and pavement damage using just your phone’s camera. Designed for contractors, councils, and infrastructure professionals, PaveVision helps streamline inspections, save costs, and improve public safety.

Key Features

Automatic Damage Detection

AI-driven detection of cracks, potholes, and road faults with instant identification.

Detailed Damage Reports

Generate comprehensive reports with photos, locations, and repair recommendations.

Analytics Dashboard

Track trends, prioritize repairs, and make data-driven decisions over time.
