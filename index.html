CoreSurface is a leading roading and infrastructure company focused on modern, efficient, and high-performance surfacing solutions. PaveVision – A CoreSurface Company

PaveVision is our flagship mobile AI solution for identifying and reporting road damage using a smartphone. Designed for local authorities, contractors, and infrastructure teams, it turns everyday devices into powerful inspection tools — reducing cost, time, and effort.

AI Road Analysis

Scan, detect, and report potholes, cracks, and surface defects in real time using the phone’s camera and CoreSurface’s proprietary AI model.

Contractor-Ready

Export condition reports, generate work orders, and track asset condition through the cloud dashboard.

Easy Deployment

No need for special equipment — just a smartphone, internet, and a vehicle.
