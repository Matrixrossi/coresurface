About CoreSurface

We are a UK-based civil engineering and surfacing contractor, delivering next-generation solutions for highways, local authorities, private estates, and commercial clients. From asphalt laying to high-tech pavement diagnostics, CoreSurface represents quality, safety, and future-focused delivery.

Our Core Services

Road Resurfacing

Comprehensive bituminous and asphalt surfacing for carriageways, motorways, and private networks. Built to specification and regulation.

Pothole & Patch Repairs

Rapid-response surface defect correction and structural patching — minimizing downtime and maximising durability.

Industrial Surfacing

Full surfacing and construction for warehousing, logistic hubs, forecourts and retail premises. Built for HGV and heavy-duty traffic.

Asset Assessment & Condition Surveys

Using our in-house AI tech, we digitally assess road surface quality and degradation patterns across highways and estates.

PaveVision – A CSF Company

PaveVision is CoreSurface's proprietary pavement condition technology — built to deliver real-time, visual diagnostics from any smartphone. Designed for councils, engineers, and contractors, it enables instant detection of road damage, including potholes, cracks, and rutting.

Benefits: Real-time reporting • Exportable condition maps • Integrates with asset management systems • Minimal training required
